# Emery Berger Style Guides

Style guidelines for writing and presenting, distilled from award-winning computer science papers and conference talks by [Emery Berger](https://emeryberger.com).

## Contents

- **[EmeryBergerStyleGuide.md](EmeryBergerStyleGuide.md)** — Writing style guide covering voice, tone, abstract structure, sentence-level craft, figures, and evaluation presentation. Based on papers including Hoard, DieHard, Coz, Scalene, ChatDBG, and others.

- **[EmeryBergerWritingVoice.md](EmeryBergerWritingVoice.md)** — A deeper analysis of writing voice patterns, covering rhetorical posture, macro-structure, sentence-level fingerprints, and vocabulary tendencies.

- **[Presentations.md](Presentations.md)** — Presentation design guidelines covering slide format, typography, color, visuals, and narrative structure. Based on talks including *Performance Matters*, *Mesh* (CppCon 2019), and *Powered by AI* (CppCon 2023).
